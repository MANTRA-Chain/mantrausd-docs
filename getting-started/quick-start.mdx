## Prerequisites

- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Rabby, Phantom, etc.)
- Some OM tokens for gas fees (you can acquire OM tokens by bridging from any EVM chain via [Squid](https://app.squidrouter.com/))

## Acquire mantraUSD

You can acquire mantraUSD through:

**Option 1: Bridge funds via Squid directly into mantraUSD**

Bridge your funds from other chains directly into mantraUSD using [Squid](https://app.squidrouter.com/).

**Option 2: Bridge funds via Hyperlane and then swap to mantraUSD via QuickSwap or Lotus DEX**

1. Bridge your funds to MANTRA Chain using [Hyperlane](https://nexus.hyperlane.xyz/?destination=mantra)
2. Swap to mantraUSD using [QuickSwap](https://quickswap.exchange/) or [Lotus DEX](https://lotusdex.io/)

<Info>
  mantraUSD is an extension of $M from M0, with all collateralization handled by M0's infrastructure. To mint new mantraUSD tokens, you'll need to work through M0's minting process. See [Minting & Burning](/how-it-works/minting-burning) for details.
</Info>

## Use mantraUSD

Once you have mantraUSD, you can:

- **Send Payments**: Transfer mantraUSD to other addresses
- **Use in DeFi**: Provide liquidity, stake, or use in other DeFi protocols
- **Store Value**: Hold mantraUSD as a stable store of value
